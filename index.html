About the Workshop

Join us for an exciting day of creative coding where beginners and intermediate coders come together to explore web development, generative art, and interactive design!

Whether you're a student, professional, or curious beginner, this workshop offers a relaxed and supportive space to learn new skills, meet others, and build something fun.

Workshop Details

  • Date: Saturday, July 20, 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • Location: Innovation Hub, City Centre
  • Hosted by: Code for Creatives

Agenda

  1. 10:00 – Welcome & Introduction
  2. 10:30 – Creative Coding Basics
  3. 12:00 – Lunch Break (provided)
  4. 1:00 – Hands-on Coding Session
  5. 3:00 – Group Showcase

What to Bring

  • A laptop (with charger)
  • Notebook and pen
  • Your creativity and curiosity!
